# China Regulatory Spectrum Notes

RegionId: `CN`

Primary source:

- Ministry of Industry and Information Technology, Order No. 62,
  `中华人民共和国无线电频率划分规定`, effective 2023-07-01:
  <https://www.miit.gov.cn/gyhxxhb/jgsj/cyzcyfgs/bmgz/wxdl/art/2023/art_1e98823e689f42ca9ed14dcb6feec07a.html>

Phase 8 Tier 1 implements source-backed approximations for:

- AM medium-wave broadcast,
- VHF FM broadcast,
- DTMB-like terrestrial television waveform approximation,
- public mobile LTE/NR-style services including 700 MHz, 1.8 GHz,
  2.6 GHz, 3.3-3.6 GHz, 4.8-5.0 GHz, and 6.425-7.125 GHz IMT-style bands,
- 2.4 GHz and 5.8 GHz WLAN/ISM/SRD-style services,
- VHF/UHF land mobile and short-range devices.

The catalog records service and allocation evidence from the official
allocation rule. Modulation choices are standard/engineering mappings onto
the repository's available waveform families.
